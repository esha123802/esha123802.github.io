WOODLAND FOREST

Are you looking for a rustic wedding but not necessarily the country/farm theme? You might like the woodland forest aesthetic! Woodland forest weddings have a natural atmosphere, utilizing large trees, wooded areas and nature-based decor. he overall decor for the ceremony is simple and natural. Oak logs, white floral, and white wedding chairs. The woodland forest wedding is another take on the always popular rustic wedding. A woodland forest wedding relies on the natural elements of the venue, and nature-based decor to bring out the theme. Greenery, wildflowers, and oaks logs are all great elements to include in a woodland wedding. Woodland forest weddings remind me of the fairytale princess meeting her prince in the enchanted forest!
